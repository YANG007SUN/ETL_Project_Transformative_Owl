Cute as a button and stocked full of charm, don't miss this lovely bungalow on a sleepy cul de sac in North San Pedro! As you walk up to the front of the home, you are met with sweet verandas and walkways to plant to your hearts content! The living space has nice laminate flooring, and a GREAT den/office/3rd bedroom/gym that was added on (non permitted and not included in the square footage) from the back part of the garage. The kitchen has been remodeled with Granite Counter tops, custom cabinetry, Stainless Steel appliances and a sweet in kitchen dining nook with large pantry. Laundry room and powder room back up to finish off this traditional floor plan living space. The living room opens up to a back patio great for barbequing or entertaining! Upstairs you will find 2 good sized bright bedrooms with another bathroom in between. The 2 car garage has been mostly converted to the den, but still has plenty of space for work benches, storage or extra space. Recessed lighting has been installed and a newer sewer line as well. Close proximity to the 110 freeway, Gaffey's shops and restaurants and down town San Pedro!
